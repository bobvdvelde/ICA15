Wonder Who's connected to who at ICA 2015?

You may wonder how central you are, your university is, or your favourite division. You can now find out!

Universities

Unfortunately, university information was not nicely structured and may therefore contain errors. Please shoot me a mail or raise a github issue if you think something went wrong.

You may be wondering who's who in terms of universities contributing to ICA 2015. This network visualization shows you the big and well-connected actors, but also allows you to explore who your university's partners are collaborating with!

Authors

Perhaps the institutional nature of the university network isn't of interest to you. The author network shows you who's collaborating with whom. Although a bit hairy, you can search for authors (such as yourself), and explore the network from there!

View Network »

Divisions

The ICA has divisions to group people by focus. You may be interested to know to what extent other divisions overlap. The divisions are coloured based on an automated community detection, which provides some hints about the degree to which ICA is segmented in sub-groups based on divisions.
